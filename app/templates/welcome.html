Welcome - Phoenix IO


Where am I?


The nest of the Phoenix of course!
Oh, yeah that's not very informative. Phoenix IO is a project that I began myself in March of 2020. The idea came to me one morning as I scrolled through endless headlines, all of them either trying to make some sense out of the COVID-19 crisis, provide some distraction from it, or somewhere in between.
I'm typically the opposite of paranoid. I believe that if you're going to channel your own life and energy into a problem or something you feel is a threat to you, then it should all go towards solution oriented thoughts, behaviors, and actions.
Just because I'm not paranoid however, does not mean I lack awareness. As a developer, information hoarder, and leisurely watcher of all things joyous or not, I saw what was coming as soon as the signs were there.
And I saw that it was coming not because I watched the media, or read all the news articles. It was because I actually ignored all of that and focused my attention on the most empirically sourced data before allowing myself exposure to external opinions, biases, and assumptions.
Well this is a powerful approach to staying informed and empowered. Take control of your exposure to information, you are the one who your instincts and intuition can speak to. You are the only one who can listen.


The intention here is (as I sit here, coding this web application in its infancy), to create a place where like minded individuals will hopefully end up and assimilate. There's alot I can and am going to do to ensure that the flame of humanity flickers on (if even atleast for a few) and it is here where you can either help, learn, or otherwise hope.

Interested? Still confused?

I implore you, take a look around and learn of my designs
